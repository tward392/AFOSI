Supposition alpha by Jesse Klaasse and Joel Birch

A work-in-progress demonstration of optional new functionality added to Superfish via a new plugin-plugin that might end up being called “Supposition”.

This page shows that the positioning of the submenus works when the menu is located high enough in the document for scrolling to not be necessary to see it. Resize your browser window tight against the bottom and right edges of the menu then hover over the furthest right menu item and the subs contained within. Notice how the submenus are repositioned left and up so that they don’t appear outside the window. Now scroll down and tighten the window further to see the main bug begin to show itself.
